Press H for Help Menu
Press P to restart
Press Escape to Quit

RTS Mode:
1 to select Speeder
2 to select Fighter
3 to select Devastator

Left click to place them down, drag to change their direction
Right click to cancel
Scroll Wheel to zoom In and Out

In Ship:
Shift to increase thrust
Ctrl to decrease thrust
W to pitch down
S to pitch up
A to yaw left
D to yaw right
Left click to shoot
ALT to toggle camera lock and off


In Station:
Press E to interact with objects and NPC

In shop: 
Left click to select and buy
Escape to go back
